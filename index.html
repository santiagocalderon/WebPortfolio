About Me

Greetings, I'm Santiago Calderon, a seasoned Electrical Engineering student at the University of Florida, set to graduate in Spring '24. My expertise converges at the nexus of Power Systems, Electromagnetics, and Machine Learning, augmented by a diverse skill set and hands-on experiences.

Technical Skills

  • Programming Languages: Proficient in Python (PyTorch, TensorFlow), C++, Java, HTML, JS, CSS.
  • Power Systems: Specialized in Power Converter and Rectifier Analysis/Design, Motor/Generator Analysis/Design.
  • Modeling Software: Proficient in AutoCAD, SolidWorks.
  • FPGA Programming: VHDL.
  • Communication Protocols: Mastery in SPI, I2C, and Bit Banging.
  • Control Systems: Proficient in MATLAB and Control Systems Designer.
  • Signal Processing: Expert in Analog/Digital Filter Analysis/Design.
  • Digital Logic: Skilled in Quartus HDL Design.
  • PCB Design: Expertise in LTSpice, Altium.

Equipment Exposure

  • Proficient in the usage of Oscilloscopes, Power Supplies, Waveform Generators, Multimeters, PCB Soldering, 3D-Printers, Dremels/Grinders/Sanders, Lathes, TIG Welding, Vertical/Horizontal/Jig Saws, Milling Machines.
